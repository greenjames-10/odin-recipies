Bar-B-Que Chicken Pizza

Ingredients

  • Dough for one large pizza
  • 1 1/2 cups cooked chicken, , shredded rotisserie or about 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/4 large red onion, very thinly sliced
  • fresh cilantro, to taste

How to Make BBQ Chicken Pizza:

  1. Spread pizza crust dough with a thin layer of BBQ sauce. (I pre-bake my homemade pizza dough for 6 minutes before adding toppings.)
  2. Add cooked, shredded chicken to a bowl and cover it with bbq sauce.
  3. Top pizza with 1 cup of mozzarella cheese, sliced red onion, and shredded rotisserie chicken.
  4. Add remaining cup of cheese, and sprinkle with cilantro, to taste.
  5. Bake at 450 F for 10 -15 minutes or until crust is golden brown and toppings are bubbly.
